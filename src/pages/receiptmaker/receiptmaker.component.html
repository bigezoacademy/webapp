e-Receipt Maker

What the App does

This app helps a business to track its sales by recording details of every receipt created, in an online Google sheet. This means to create a receipt one requires internet connection. This has advantages over time:

  • BENEFITS

  • Build your client database gradually
  • Reliably track your revenue ----- Even without your employees knowing
  • No more need for receipt books
  • Save Money ----- buy once, use forever
